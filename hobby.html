Sketching

Sketching is one of my favorite hobbies because it allows me to express my creativity and capture the beauty of the world around me. Sketching started with charcoal tools and transformed into pencil sketching with advancement in technology and tools.

Why Sketching?

  • Allows me to display my thoughts.
  • Helps me observe and appreciate details.
  • Allows me to improve my artistic skills.
  • Provides a sense of relaxation and mindfulness.

Poetry

Poetry is a wonderful way for me to explore my emotions, thoughts, and experiences. It gives me a unique platform to communicate and connect with others through words.

Why Poetry?

  • It's a great way to express thoughts and feelings. It allows a person to build perspectives.
  • Allows me to express myself creatively.
  • Offers a means to explore and understand complex emotions.
  • Provides an outlet for self-reflection and personal growth.

Example:

The moon, like a glowing pearl in the sky,
Illuminates the night with its silver light.
Whispers of the wind carry secrets untold,
As I pen my thoughts in verses of gold.
